Our Story

Play above the cut.

High Cut Golf began with a simple spark of innovation when Alex Dordoni developed BRDTEES, a line of unique 3D printed golf tees designed for precision. Realizing the potential for something bigger, Alex brought in his brother Chris and cousin Mike. Together, they brainstormed a vision that evolved into High Cut Golf—a brand dedicated to the everyday golfer who wants to look and feel like a PGA pro.

"We believe that your gear should match your ambition."

Whether you're fighting for a birdie or just trying to keep it in the short grass, our mission is to provide premium apparel and accessories that help you stand out. At High Cut Golf, we don't just sell products; we cultivate a mindset for those who strive to play above the cut every single round.
