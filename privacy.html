The Clubhouse Rules

At High Cut Golf, we’re better at protecting your data than we are at protecting par. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you visit gethighcutgolf.com.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Addresses: If you join "The Gallery" for early access to drops, we collect your email address. We use this strictly to send you updates about our products and the occasional self-deprecating golf joke.
  • Usage Data: Our hosting providers (Vercel and Cloudflare) may collect basic technical data like your IP address and browser type to ensure the website stays live and secure.

2. Cookies

We use essential cookies to make the site work. By staying on the site, you're cool with it. These cookies help us understand how many people are visiting so we know how many hats to order.

3. Third-Party Services

We don't sell your data to third parties. We only share information with the tools required to run this operation, like our email service provider and our hosting platforms.

4. Your Rights

You can opt out of our emails at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any message. If you want us to delete your email from our records entirely, just reach out.
